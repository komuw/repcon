Mission and Vision:

Repcon Associates is a Kenyan owned company fully registered under the Companies Act.
The name Repcon is an abbreviation for Resource and Environmental Planning Consulting Associates, which in a nutshell, captures the business focus for the company.
The company was founded and registered in 1998 with the aim of offering homegrown expertise and solutions to resource planning and environmental management challenges in Kenya and the Region.
Though registered in 1998, the firm brought together the cumulative experiences of the partners who had been in consultancy since the early 1990.
Originally established as a partnership operating from an apartment building, we have expanded to a fully-fledged consultancy firm located its own Training Resource Center at Muguga- the research capital of Kenya.

The original focus of the firm was forestry related consultancy services. However, with time and in order to meet increasing demand, the firm has progressively expanded activities to cover the entire spectrum of Environment and Natural Resource Development.
Under this focus, we have specialized in provision of research, consultancy and training services for sustainable environmental management. The company maintains an up-to-date data bank comprising a wide selection of literature and data covering the entire realm of environmental practice including standards of quality and this guarantees quick synthesis and production of quality outputs and reports on any of the relevant sub-sectors.
Our firm therefore has all the necessary capacity to deliver and we are committed to satisfying our clients' requirements.
We adopt a participatory approach to all assignments to ensure that clients are satisfied and fully identify with the outputs.

Our goal as Repcon Associates is to satisfy demands and requirements of our clients through provision of quality services. We adopt a participatory approach to all assignments to ensure that clients are satisfied and fully identify with the outputs.

Statutory Compliance:

Repcon Associates pursues business practice that is competitive, socially and economically acceptable as borne by compliance with laws reigning on the land. The Company is legally registered in Kenya and is legally compliant with all laws including requirement for prompt filling of tax returns.

Professional accreditation:

Repcon Associates is accredited with NEMA as Firm of Experts No 0002.
Repcon Associates is also accredited to the Kenya Institute of Environmental Impact Assessment The Core Principal at Repcon Associates Mr Michael Wairagu is accredited to the National Environmental Management Council - NEMC of Tanzania.

Human Resources:

Repcon Associates brings together the combined experiences and skills of its founders. All partners have been closely involved in development programmes for the last two decades whereby, coming from different disciplines and experiences, they spearhead implementation of innovative approaches to development thinking namely participatory frameworks that enhance dialogue, forge partnerships and encourage conflict management and resolution. These efforts put strong emphasis on small-scale and local solutions targeted at eliminating bottlenecks to sustainable development- a bottom-up, beneficiary oriented approach, coupled with intensive hands-on training alongside capacity building for grassroots and indigenous knowledge bases targeting effective use and management of limited funds, improve the chance for achieving sustainable results. Michael Wairagu: Environment and Social Safeguards Specialist:
Wairagu is an Environmentalist with over 27 years' experience in development planning in the sectors of water, forestry, ASAL and general environmental conservation. Mr. Wairagu has extensive experience in providing consulting services to government funded projects, bilateral and multilateral agencies in project development, Environmental assessments, and capacity building and policy analysis.
